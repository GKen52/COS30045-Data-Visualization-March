Buying Tips

  • Check the Energy Rating Label - more stars = lower costs
  • Choose the right size - larger screens use more power
  • LED TVs are more efficient than plasma
  • Look for smart power management features

Standby Power

TVs in standby use 0.5-3 watts. Turn off at the wall to save $1-8 per year.
