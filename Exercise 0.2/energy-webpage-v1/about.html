About Energy Saver

Our Mission

We help Australian households understand and reduce their energy consumption. Making informed appliance choices saves money and reduces environmental impact.

Australian Energy Facts

  • Average household: 15-20 kWh per day
  • Average electricity rate: $0.25-$0.35 per kWh
  • Heating/cooling: 40% of energy use
  • Appliances: 30% of energy use
  • Standby power: up to 10% of usage
